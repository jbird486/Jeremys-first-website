Study Strategies

Time to work hard, play harder

🗂️

Past Papers First

Work through NESA past papers under exam conditions from Term 3, Year 12. Nothing beats knowing the question style cold.

📆

Spaced Repetition

Review content at increasing intervals — after 1 day, 3 days, 1 week, 2 weeks. Use Anki flashcards for formulas and definitions.

✍️

Active Recall

Don't just re-read notes. Close the book and write out everything you remember. Test yourself ruthlessly — it's how memory forms.

🤝

Study Groups

Explaining concepts to peers is one of the most powerful learning tools. If you can teach it, you know it. Form groups of 3–4.

📊

Track Your Weak Spots

Keep an error log — every time you get something wrong, write down why. Revisit your log weekly and drill those specific areas.

⏱️

Time Management

For exams, allocate roughly 1.5× the marks in minutes (e.g. 6-mark question = ~9 min). Practice pacing with timed mock exams.

🧘

Rest & Recovery

Sleep is non-negotiable — consolidation happens overnight. Aim for 8 hrs, and schedule proper breaks to avoid burnout before trials.

🔗

Connect Concepts

The HSC rewards synthesis. Build concept maps linking topics across and within subjects — examiners love seeing connected thinking.
